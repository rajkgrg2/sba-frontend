Nepal is second richest country in the world after Brasil in terms of natural resources. It has vast greet forests, great himalayas and a flat terrain. It has world heritage sites. According to the UNESCO, two that are in cultural categories are Kathmandu valley( city of temples) and Lumbini birthplace of Buddha, naturally, Sagarmatha and Chitwan national parks. There are evidence of various ancient architecture in carving woods, sculpture, paintings. Site seing of himalayas in the morning brings happyness and peace in mind. It has variety of outdoor activities to the tourists. Such as trekkiing, white water rafting, kayaking, cascading, birdwatching, jungle safary.

Trecking
Trecking is available all year around but it will be best in autumn season, which starts from Sep-Nov. In winter, from Dec-Feb, it is not good to go in high altitude areas approx. 2700m. In spring, March to May, it is very good season for trecking as there'll be Rhododendron all over the hilly mountain areas but temperature of Terai(lower part of Nepal) will be hot and recommend not to travel. In Summer, June- August, it is alwasys better to travel in this season to see all the green forests in all over the valleys and mountains.
Trecking Cost
Trecking cost is very cheaper in Nepal comparing to other countries. It depends on your choice of using facilities and food and services. Nepali food (dal, bhat and tarkari) is available for only $1-$2. You can get guide for about $20-$30 per day and potters $5-$15 per day. If you are interesting in going to jungle safary in various national parks around the country, there are fee from $5 to $25 depending on the types of national parks. Moutaineering
Everybody has to get permit to go to moutaineering. Mountaineering is among the most important for the tourists attraction in Nepal. There are more than 325 different peaks in Nepal that are available for the climbing including the top of the world The Mt. Everest. There have been competition in breaking records by climbing Mt. everest in various areas after Tenzing Sherpa of Nepal and Sir Edmund Hillary of New Zealand have first conquired the great peak.Those who want to see himalayas from close range but scared of climbing, there is another option. Paragliding, this is available in Pokhara. It'll take close to the annapurana himalayan range, so that tourist can take full advantage of taking closer view of mountain without climbing.
There are four airelines which have mountain flight packages to go around and over great himalayas including the Mt. Everest. All flights depart from the capital of Nepal Kathmandu. The cost of flights are range from US$120 -$150 by airelines. But helicopter are more expensive price range from US$2000-$4000. Since Nepal is made of tall mountains and valleys, the flights are very dangerous in unconditional or bad weathers. So, it is always nice to go in mountain flights from February to april and September and October as this are the best months for the good weather.

Rafting/ Kayaking/ Canyoning
Rafting is famous in Nepal. Every year there are numerous tourists comes to take advantage of white rivers of Nepal. There are numerous rivers that are offord for the rafting. The rivers are comes from snow melting from himalayas and flowing towards the low level terai through the vast green forests. Canyoning is also available in Nepal. Jumping, sliding and climbing along waterfalls is great as rafting.
Rafting is also considered as the third most popular and challenging sport in Nepal. There are numerous rivers available for rafting ranging from rate 1 to 6 according to the speed of the rivers current. It is very challenging sport as the speed of river current is very different from place to place from its identical geological shape of the lands and mountains. Those rivers with rate more than 3 are considered very dangerous for the new rookie, but there are training available for everybody before experiencing this great adventure. There's nothing to be worry as trainings are given in different places and different times as your availability and wish. Once you have taken the training, it is very easier and safe to go even to those rivers which have rate more than 3.

Jungle Safary
Jungle safary is considered forth famour adventurous sports in Nepal after river rafting, which attracts numorous foreign tourists as well as domestic tourists in those designated areas covered by national parks. You can experience the natural adventures in those conservation and national parks such as bird watching, elephant riding, sightseeing of great himalayas etc. Each natinal parks are famouse for different activities such as Chitawan national parks is famouse for the varieties species of animals and birds and Bardia national park is famouse for the great jungle adventure.
